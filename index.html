Let Your Palate Be Your Guide

Travel the World in Your Glass

Open a bottle and let the aroma and flavors fulfill your sense of wanderlust. Whatever the grape, wherever the place, wine has a way of delivering the world. From California to France, the choice of destination is yours — your passion for exploring the world starts here.



FLEUR DE MER 2017 Côtes de Provence Rosé

Grown in the rolling hills and valleys of Provence, where fields of lavender thrive in the warm sun and fresh, crisp seaside air, Fleur de Mer means “flower of the sea.” This brilliant, pale pink Rosé is beautifully balanced and round with ripe fruit notes — like red cherry, raspberry, white peach, and subtle hints of grapefruit — with tangy acidity and hints of lavender and Mediterranean herbs. This elegant Provençal Rosé is the perfect bottle to elevate your next barbecue or summer gathering with friends. Slip on a pair of oversized sunglasses and prepare to be transported to southern France — you deserve it.
WHITEHAVEN 2017 Marlborough Sauvignon Blanc

It’s no wonder that this Sauvignon Blanc commands summer. Thanks to an exceptional vintage, the 2017 Whitehaven is superbly structured and balanced, capturing bright citrus notes of grapefruit and lemongrass, plus nuances of fresh-snipped grass and warm pear nectar with notes of tropical fruit. Crafted by a family winery, whose winemaker’s care and attention yields critically-acclaimed Sauvignon Blancs, this wine is vibrant, fruit forward, and boasts a long, clean finish. Enjoy it while gazing upon a starry night, mimicking the endless skies of New Zealand’s South Island.
J VINEYARDS & WINERY 2016 California Pinot Gris

Long known as one of California’s top sparkling and varietal wine producers, J Vineyards & Winery’s Pinot Gris truly delights. Grown in Sonoma County, renowned for farm-to-table dining against the naturally stunning backdrop of sun-soaked hills, this wine is designed for laid back hospitality at its finest. On the nose are notes of candied pineapple, lemongrass, white peach, and jasmine. This wine is fruit forward with Anjou pear, yellow apple, and complex notes of orange blossom and a sliver of minerality on the palate. Serve it with anything from Manchego cheese and peach chutney to an all-time J favorite: seared scallops drizzled in a pineapple brown butter sauce.
WILLIAM HILL ESTATE 2016 North Coast Sauvignon Blanc

Selected from a variety of coastal vineyards, this wine embodies the very best of California living: pushing boundaries, discovering amazing new places, and living with intention. The highly fragrant North Coast Sauvignon Blanc offers aromas of citrus and honeysuckle. On the palate come zesty notes of Meyer lemon, lime leaf, ripe pear, and hints of peach. Balanced, smooth, round, and full of well developed citrus, stone fruit, and tropical notes, this wine is clean and refreshingly crisp on the finish. This is truly the ultimate California lifestyle wine.
